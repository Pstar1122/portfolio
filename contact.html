Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply browse our course catalog, select a course, and click "Enroll Now". You'll need to create an account if you haven't already.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with your course.

Can I access courses on mobile?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully optimized for mobile devices including phones and tablets.

How do I get a certificate?

After completing a course and passing the final exam, you'll automatically receive a certificate you can download and share.
